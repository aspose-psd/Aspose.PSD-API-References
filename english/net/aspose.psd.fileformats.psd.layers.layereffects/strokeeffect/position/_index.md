## StrokeEffect.Position property

Gets or sets the position of the stroke effect to control the alignment of your stroke to the PSD layer content. The value can be Inside to draw stroke inside of the PSD layer content, or Outside to draw stroke around of PSD layer content, and Center to draw stroke both inside and outside.
