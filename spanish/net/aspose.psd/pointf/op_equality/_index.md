```csharp
public static bool operator ==(PointF point1, PointF point2)
```

| Parámetro | Escribe | Descripción |
| --- | --- | --- |
| point1 | PointF | Un primero[`PointF`](../) comparar. |
| point2 | PointF | Un segundo[`PointF`](../) comparar. |
